MOBIS Electrification Plant in Savannah, USA

  

  Currently, MOBIS operates seven plants and two R&D centers in China. The Chinese plants produce a wide range of modules and core components, while maintaining a close partnership with major Chinese automakers. In terms of R&D, the research centers in Shanghai and Yantai play a central role. They develop technologies tailored to the needs of Chinese consumers while responding swiftly to rapidly changing technological trends.  

  India is a region where demand for SUVs and electric vehicles is growing rapidly, and MOBIS's research center in Hyderabad serves as a base for developing technological solutions to address these market changes. The center conducts research not only to support vehicle production in India but also in various software fields such as platform-based software development and integrated control systems. In 2023, MOBIS established a battery system plant in Indonesia, a key country in the ASEAN region, positioning this plant as a key strategic base for expanding its electrification ecosystem.
